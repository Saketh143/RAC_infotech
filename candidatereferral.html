Candidate Refferal Bonus

If you know someone who would be a good fit for positions advertised on RAC InfoTech’s company website (www.racinfotek.com), please share the job description with them. If they are interested, complete the Candidate Referral Form and send in your referral’s resume along with the candidate referral form to info@racinfotek.com. You can also just have your referral send us their resume with your name listed as the referrer. Once a referral entry form is submitted, the candidate will be entered into our database and remain active for six months. If your candidate is not hired within 6 months of being referred, that referral is no longer eligible for a bonus. A candidate cannot be referred more than once. The successful candidate must be employed by RAC INFOTECH for a period of 90 workdays in order for you (the referrer) to be eligible for the bonus
